A Very Confusing Card Game

Game Log

Rules

The game is kind of played liked War, but dumber. The goal is to win all the cards from your opponent.

The Player is dealt 3 cards. Choose one of the cards to play and the computer will randomly select a card to oppose it. The player who wins takes both cards.

Which Card Wins? The only way to find out is to play.

When your pile of cards is empty you'll shuffle all the cards you've won into your new draw pile.

If you play a card that has not been named you will need to name it.

If you play a match up that has never been played you decide which card wins, and you decide why!
